How to Use the Dashboard (For Departments)

  • Filter by Department: Use the dropdown to focus only on your department's service data.
  • Track Applications: View total applications, open, delivered, rejected, and overdue counts.
  • Drill Down: Analyze performance by district, office, or service name.
  • Timeliness: Monitor delivery and wait time performance trends.

If your data isn't loading, try refreshing the page or zooming out your browser view (Ctrl + -).
