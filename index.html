🔍 Sewa Setu Service Delivery Dashboard Instructional Guide (2021-10 to 2025-03)

🧾 1. Summary KPIs (Top Row)

This section provides high-level performance indicators:

  • # Applications – Total number of applications received through Sewa Setu (17.5 million).
  • # Delivered – Successfully delivered services (886,162).
  • # Rejected – Applications that were rejected (289,171).
  • # Returned – Applications returned for correction or additional input (74,841).
  • # Open – Applications currently pending (170,051).
  • # Overdue – Applications open beyond their SLA/due date (114,406).

📂 2. Filters (Below KPIs)

These dropdowns allow users to slice and dice data:

  • Department / Service
  • Is RTPS Service?
  • Integration Type
  • Source Application
  • Mode of Application
  • Date Range (From / To)

Use these filters to analyze trends or problems at a granular level.

🏢 3. Department Overview

Shows the number of applications per department:

  • #Svc(Txn) – Number of services provided by the department.
  • #Appln – Total applications received.
  • % of Appln – Contribution to total applications.
  • #Open – Current pending applications.
  • #Overdue – Open applications that have crossed SLA/due dates.

🔎 Useful to identify high-load or underperforming departments.

🧾 4. Service Overview

Similar to the Department Overview, but at the service level:

  • service_name – Name of the individual service.
  • #Appln – Applications received for that service.
  • #Open – Currently open.
  • #Overdue – Overdue applications.

👉 Shows the services contributing to the most applications or delays (e.g., e-Khajana or Jamabandi copies).

🕒 5. Service Delivery Speed (Past Performance)

Bar graph showing past performance of delivery times:

  • A – Under 10 Days: 71.7%
  • B–F – Longer durations, visualized in descending performance

⚠️ Helps identify delays or inefficiencies.

📊 6. Trend of Application Receipts

Bar graph showing the monthly trend of application submissions:

  • Peaks in specific months (e.g., early 2023)
  • Average line helps benchmark fluctuations

🎯 Use this to prepare for seasonal surges.

🕒 7. Current Pendency of Open Applications

Shows breakdown of currently open applications by aging:

  • A: < 10 Days – 4.8%
  • F: > 1 Year – 27.6%
  • 🔥 Major bottleneck areas: 23.5% pending for 31–90 days, and 39.5% over 91 days (RED zones).

🌍 8. District Overview

Shows geographic breakdown:

  • #Open – Total pending in the district.
  • #Overdue – Pending beyond SLA.
  • % Overdue – Portion of overdue applications in the district.

🔍 Identify poorly performing districts (e.g., Dhubri, Kamrup, Dibrugarh).

🏢 9. Office Overview

Drills further into specific offices:

  • Subdistrict offices or departments
  • Number of open and overdue applications

📌 Helps track local office performance for targeted improvements.

✅ Actionable Use Cases

  • 📍 Department-level accountability: Target departments with high overdue rates.
  • 📍 Service improvement: Re-engineer services like e-Khajana with >25,000 overdue cases.
  • 📍 District administration alerts: Prioritize Dhubri, Kamrup, and Bongaigaon for process revamps.
  • 📍 Citizen SLA improvements: Monitor and improve applications pending over 90 days.

🔍 Dashboard Overview (2021-10 to 2025-03)

This dashboard tracks the service delivery performance of the Revenue & Disaster Management Department under the Sewa Setu portal for the selected timeframe.

🧩 Key Metrics

  • # Applications: 7,690,152
  • # Delivered: 465,593
  • # Rejected: 231,331
  • # Returned: 21,641
  • # Open: 138,812
  • # Overdue: 88,976

🏢 Department Overview

42 services contributed 7.69 million applications. 138K still pending, and nearly 89K overdue. Full focus is on this department due to the filter applied.

📋 Top Services with High Volume or Delay

  • 📄 Settlement of Khas and Ceiling Surplus Land: 1.4M applications, 18.9K open.
  • 📄 Certified Copy of Jamabandi: 1M+, 7K overdue.
  • 📄 e-Khajana: 960K received, 25K overdue — highest concern.

⏱️ Delivery Speed

  • 59.6% of services delivered within 10 days.
  • ~14% took more than 3 months (E+F).

📈 Application Trend

Peak: Feb–Mar 2023 with 900K+ applications/month. Decline and stabilization below average in recent months.

📊 Pendency Analysis

  • 31.1% pending: 31–90 days
  • 42.1% pending: 91–365 days
  • 16.3% pending: >1 year
  • ⚠️ 58.4% applications pending >3 months

🌍 District View

  • Dhubri: 11.4K open, 8.1K overdue
  • Kamrup, Dibrugarh also high volume

🏢 Office View

  • Circle Office Dispur: 2,761 open, 100% overdue
  • HQ: Almost all 2,781 applications overdue

✅ Recommendations

  • Target e-Khajana for rapid clearance
  • Focus on Dispur, Tezpur, Srijangram offices
  • District escalation: Dhubri and Kamrup
  • Use proactive alerts or automation to avoid >90 day pendency
